Runaway Meatball Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 30, 2026

Runaway Meatball is a one-tap pixel arcade game where players dodge pasta forks, collect coins and powerups, complete the RUNAWAY MEATBALL letter set, choose backgrounds, and unlock cosmetic hats. This policy explains what information is stored or used by the app.

Data Stored On Your Device

The game stores gameplay progress locally on your device so the game can remember your choices and progress. This local data may include:

  • Best score and challenge best score
  • Coin count
  • Unlocked hats and equipped hat
  • Selected background
  • Collected RUNAWAY MEATBALL letters
  • Tutorial state and sound/mute preference

This local gameplay progress is not sent to a developer-operated server. If you delete the app or clear local app/site data, this progress may be removed.

Accounts, Purchases, And User Content

Runaway Meatball does not currently include account login, social login, direct messaging, user-generated content, or in-app purchases. Hats, coins, backgrounds, scores, and letters are gameplay features stored locally on the device.

Ads And Third Parties

The iPhone version may show bottom banner ads through Google AdMob. Google may receive information needed to request, deliver, limit, personalize if enabled, and measure ads. Depending on the app settings, device settings, consent choices, and Google's SDK behavior, this may include device identifiers, advertising identifiers, IP address or approximate location derived from network information, ad interactions, usage data, and diagnostic data.

The developer does not currently operate a separate analytics service for Runaway Meatball. Ad-related collection is handled by Google AdMob and should be reviewed alongside the ad settings used for the App Store build.

Tracking And Consent

If the submitted app build uses personalized ads, the advertising identifier, or tracking across apps and websites, the app should request consent where required and follow Apple's App Tracking Transparency rules before accessing the advertising identifier. If the app is configured for non-personalized ads only, the App Store privacy answers should match that configuration.

Network Use

The bundled game assets and audio can run locally inside the app. Network access may be used for Google AdMob ads and related ad measurement, consent, fraud prevention, and delivery services. If additional online services are added later, this policy should be updated before release.

Children

The app does not knowingly collect personal information directly from children. If you believe a child has provided personal information through an ad or third-party service, contact support so the issue can be reviewed.
