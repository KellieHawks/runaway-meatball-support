Meatball Mayhem Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 30, 2026

Meatball Mayhem is designed as a simple arcade game. The game stores progress locally, and the iPhone version may display bottom banner ads through Google AdMob.

Data Stored On Your Device

The game stores local progress on your device, such as best score, coin count, unlocked hats, selected hat, and selected background. This data stays on your device and is used only to make the game work.

Analytics, Ads, And Tracking

The iPhone app may use Google AdMob to show banner ads and measure ad performance. Google may receive device, advertising, and usage information needed to request, deliver, limit, and measure ads. The game does not use social login or a developer-operated analytics account.

If personalized ads or tracking are enabled, the app should request consent where required and follow Apple's App Tracking Transparency rules before accessing the advertising identifier.

Network Use

The bundled game can run locally, but ads require network requests to Google's ad services. If additional online services are added later, this policy should be updated before release.

Children

The app does not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Contact

For support or privacy questions, contact the developer at: meatballmayhem.support@gmail.com.
