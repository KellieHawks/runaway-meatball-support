Meatball Mayhem Support

Need help with Meatball Mayhem? This page is the support home for the game.

How To Play

Tap to flap, dodge the pasta forks, collect coins, and unlock hats from the hat vending machine.

Progress Reset

Open Backgrounds from the title screen, then use Reset Progress. The reset button requires a confirmation tap.

Sound

Use the Sound button outside the fullscreen canvas shell, or the device mute controls if running inside an iOS wrapper.
