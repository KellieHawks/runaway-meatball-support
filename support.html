Runaway Meatball Support

Need help with Runaway Meatball? This page is the support home for the game.

Runaway Meatball is a one-tap pixel arcade game where a brave meatball dodges pasta forks, collects coins, grabs powerups, completes the RUNAWAY MEATBALL letter set, chooses backgrounds, and unlocks cosmetic hats.

Launch And Loading

When the game opens, a loading screen should appear immediately while bundled local game assets initialize. If the app stays on a black screen or never reaches the title screen, close and reopen the app, then email support with your device model and iOS version.

How To Play

Tap the screen to flap upward. Release to fall. Fly through fork gaps, avoid the fork prongs, and keep the run going as long as you can. You score by surviving fork sets and by collecting coins.

Collectibles And Powerups

  • Coins add to your wallet and can be spent on hat capsules.
  • Letters fill the RUNAWAY MEATBALL tracker. Gray letters have not been collected yet. When the whole phrase is complete, a bonus hat capsule opens during gameplay.
  • Shield protects the meatball from a hit while the timer is active.
  • Magnet pulls nearby pickups toward the meatball.
  • Speed boost starts a short faster-scoring run.
  • Heart adds an extra chance, up to the in-game limit.
  • Question coin throws confetti and shuffles through unlocked hats before equipping one.
  • Pause coin briefly freezes the moving fork gaps.

Hats And Backgrounds

Open Hats from the title screen to spend 5 coins on a random hat capsule, or select a locked hat and buy it directly for 10 coins. Use the arrow buttons to page through the full hat catalog. Unlocked hats can be tapped to equip them. If every hat is already unlocked, completing the letters shows an all-hats message instead of awarding another hat.

Open Backgrounds from the title screen to choose the game view. Current backgrounds include Sunset City, Night City, Kitchen, and Restaurant.

Ads

The iPhone version may show a bottom banner ad. Ads should stay outside the playable game area. The app may also offer a one-time Remove Ads purchase and a Restore option. If an ad blocks controls or content, please email support with your device model, iOS version, and a screenshot.

Sound And Pause

Use the sound button to mute or unmute game audio and adjust music volume. Use the pause button during gameplay to pause the run. The app should also pause active gameplay when it moves to the background.

Progress And Reset

The game saves progress locally on your device, including best score, coins, unlocked hats, selected hat, selected background, collected letters, tutorial state, sound/music preference, and Remove Ads state.

The reset button in Hat Vending resets hat unlocks and equips the Chef Toque. To clear all local progress, delete and reinstall the app, or clear the app/site data if you are playing a browser build.
